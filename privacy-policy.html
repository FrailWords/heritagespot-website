HeritageSpot Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2025

Introduction

HeritageSpot ("we", "our", or "us") respects the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Location Information: With your permission, we collect your device's location to provide the "Near Me" feature that helps you find heritage sites close to you. This is optional and you can disable location services at any time through your device settings.
  • Usage Information: We collect anonymous usage statistics to help us improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our services
  • Show you nearby heritage sites when you use the "Near Me" feature
  • Analyze app usage to improve user experience

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to third parties. We may share anonymous, aggregated usage data for analytics purposes.

Data Storage

Most of the app's data is stored locally on your device. Location data is only processed to provide the "Near Me" functionality and is not permanently stored on our servers.

Your Rights

You can choose not to provide certain information, such as location data, but this may limit certain features of the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

Content Rights

The content in this app regarding heritage sites, including descriptions, historical information, and some images, is sourced from Wikipedia and Wikimedia Commons under Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike (CC BY-SA) licenses. We provide proper attribution within the app for all such content (wherever applicable).

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: heritagespotin@gmail.com
