Heritage Spot App - Synopsis

What is Heritage Spot?

Heritage Spot is a comprehensive mobile app guide to India's magnificent heritage sites, temples, mosques, churches, and historical monuments. We cover over 200 carefully curated heritage sites from all regions of India - South, North, West, East, Central, and Northeast.

Who Uses Heritage Spot

Designed for: Pilgrims, tourists, history enthusiasts, and cultural explorers

  • Pilgrims planning spiritual journeys
  • Tourists (domestic and international) exploring India's culture
  • Families looking for educational weekend trips
  • History enthusiasts interested in India's past
  • Cultural explorers wanting to discover lesser-known sites

Why Now?

Heritage tourism in India is growing rapidly. More people are traveling domestically, especially for spiritual and cultural experiences. There's increasing interest among younger Indians to learn about their heritage, but they want comprehensive information that's easily accessible on their phones.

What Makes Us Different

Most heritage information is scattered across different sources, often only in English, and lacks depth. We've created one comprehensive app with everything in multiple languages, covering both famous sites and hidden gems that most people don't know about.

Content Examples

Our app includes places like:

  • Famous sites: Ajanta Caves, Golden Temple, Meenakshi Temple
  • Hidden gems: Ancient stepwells in Gujarat, Portuguese churches in Goa, heritage havelis in Rajasthan
  • All religions: Hindu temples, mosques, Christian churches, Buddhist monasteries, Sikh gurudwaras
